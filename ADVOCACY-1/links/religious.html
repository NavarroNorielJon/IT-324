What is Religious Tolerance?

In one society, many religions and cultures live and stay what they are. Beliefs that are much different from another. As it grows, some of them criticize and disrespect one another, resulting to conflicts and the endless noise every group has to argue about. In religious tolerance, everybody practices respect and civility. Every group must have the freedom to believe what they believe and respect what others believe. Making a more peaceful community and each groups equal rights.


Religious Tolerance in Palma-Urbano

Religions in the barangay are some. Mostly are Catholic and others are non-Catholic. The 2 churches in the barangay are for the Catholics while the non-Catholic groups had none. From the issues and arguments in the barangay, there are no conflicts regarding different religions. Moreover, the barangay still maintains the peacefulness of the religions and respecting each individual. One situation is when there are events for all of the people in the barangay. The officials tend to be more sensitive to other religions. Having them something they could actually and rightfully eat and Activities which other non-Catholic people can participate.


The Barangay in ACTION!

What if an issue comes up.. what will the barangay do? The barangay has what they call "LUPON" which is the judiciary sytem of the barangay. It is composed of the Lupon pres. and the barangay captain. When there is a problem or issue in the barangay, it first goes through the Lupon, where the groups can talk it out and make up. This procedure of the Lupon reduces groups to demand each other and take it to the real court.
