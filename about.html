Introduction

During 2016 Mayor Mauricio Domogan created the Baguio City Human Rights Action Center (NHRAC) and the Baguio City Human Rights Action Team (BCHRAcT) to handle human rights concern and complaints on human rights violations. Its goal is to protect human rights principles and to enable the city government to mandate, respect and fulfill human rights with right jurisdiction.

Fast In-Migration and out-migration in Barangay Palma-Urbano are active because most of the residences are students. Although the barangay is small, it is a go-to and most likely the best residence for students, tourist or families because the barangay has active businesses, 2 church, 2 schools, and a lot of transcient houses. Burnham Park and the market is near the barangay and is very accessible. The Barangay promotes Orders, Peace, and Cleanliness. Barangay Palma-Urbano's mission is to inform and encourage the community to do their responsibilities as good citizens. In todays era almost all places in our country are being abused of their rights, but if all of us do our part and do the right thing we will ultimately change and end abuses in our community. Change may be slow and doesn't come easily, but with all of our combined efforts, a little progress will be very impactful. We will someday succeed and will lead to positive change.

Human Rights in Barangay Palma-Urbano

The Right for a Clean Environment, Health and Sanitation

The United Nations had already recognized the human right to water and sanitation and acknowledged that clean drinking water and sanitation are essential to all of us. The right to clean environment is the primary advocacy of barangay Palma-Urbano because they believe that clean environment will lead a happy and healthy life. A clean environment is a Civil rights that focuses in Environmental Health and Protection. In achieving the barangay's goal to have a clean environment, its people should also do their responsibility to help protect and mantain the cleanliness in the barangay. Since the barangay is mostly compose of Transcient houses that are being occupied by students it is very relevant to educate and inform them the importance of Health and Sanitation. The barangay is very strict when it comes to garbages, the people should recycle their garbage in the right trashcan whether it is biodegradable, HIT (hazardous,, infectious, toxic), E-waste and Residual.
