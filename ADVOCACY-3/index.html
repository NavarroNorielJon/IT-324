Barangay Palma-Urbano

Barangay Palma is a small barangay with four streets namely Labsan, Urbano, Queen of Peace, and Pinpin street. The barangay is accessible to the central city district. Travelling to the town or the city hall only takes a few minutes. This means that fast in and out migration occurs. Tourists from different parts of the nation or the world often come visit the barangay for a place to stay. Despite the influx of population in the barangay, the barangay officials make sure that the place is orderly. They achieve this through incorporating their "Zero Waste" advocacy. The "Zero Waste" program discourages the people of the community to add to the garbage of our city, rather encourage them to be innovative by being creative with salvageable items (i.e. Foil satchets)and practice proper segregation.

Human Rights

Programs that the barangay practices to promote human rights:

Zero Waste

- The aim of this is to lessen garbage as opposed to generating it. They achive this by having the members of the community actively participate in properly segregating. They also enforce the members of their community to use transparent trash bags in storing their trash.

Daycare

- The Daycare is located within the same building as the barangay hall. Different activities they do on the daycare are exercises every Mondays, practice of proper nutrition, proper washing of hands and proper brushing of teeth. Which can be achieved by having the parents practice it with their children.

Banning of Cigarettes and Liquor

- Upon visiting sari sari stores within the vicinity of the barangay it can be noticed that selling of cigarettes and liquor is banned.

Food Service (Health Certificate)

- The barangay officials visit food establishments to make sure that they are working within sanitary conditions for the health of the community to avoid illnesses from spreading

Vision

A community of well-informed, empowered and ecologically concious members, living with respect and concern for each other , and are united in pushing for economic growth, social progress, and environmental protection.

Mission

The barangay shall serve with utmost integrity,transparency, and fairness in the provision of adequate basic social services and economic livelihood opportunities; and in fostering peace and order within a balanced ecology, through the active participation and cooperation of the community members, and in close coordination with other local government units, national agencies, and private institutions.

Core Values

In serving the community, the barangay shall be guided with the core values of acceptability, responsibility, commitment, integrity, and honesty that will cultivate an atmosphere of teamwork, respect and the promotion of transparency in all barangay endeavors.
