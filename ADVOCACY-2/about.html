ABOUT OUR ADVOCACY

The barangay Palma- Urbano developed an organization, The BADAC (Barangay Anti-Drug Abuse Council). The incharge of BADAC is
Kagawad Ronaldo T. Lenon, which they proposed to help reduce and strengthen the prevention of drugs in order to instill discipline
among local residents in their barangay community especially the youth.

Barangay Palma-Urbano bans the sale of liquor and cigarettes

The barangay has an ordinance on prohibiting the sale of cigarettes or tobacco products and Alcohol/Liquor or intoxicating drinks in their barangay.
That is one thing that makes the barangay stand out from all the other barangays because they are the only barangay who have this ordinance in the whole Baguio.

Violators of the developed program should be punished by a fine of P999.00 which will be paid directly to the barangay treasurer.
The cash that has been gathered will be added to the general assets of the barangay.

We can also report violators through text, just text NOYOSI to 2256. P1.00 per text for smart, globe and sun subscribers or visit www.smokefree.doh.gov.ph

MASA MASID

MASA MASID is a Mamamayan Ayaw Sa Anumalya at Mamamayan Ayaw Sa Illegal na Droga. This program is a group of volunteers that consist of seven members.
These are the volunteers that composed of constituents who are concerned about graft and corruption, and drug users.
Their task is to observe the neighborhood for any suspicious activity.

The barangay also have a box named "MASA MASID". Information like graft and corruption, possession of illegal drugs or drug dealer and drug users can be submitted
anonymously via "MASA MASID".
